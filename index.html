Analyzed codon usage, GC content, and genetic differences to understand the relationship between pathogenicity and genetic differences between two species.

Lung Cancer Risk Predictor Using Machine Learning

This Lung Cancer Risk Predictor is a basic implementation of a larger project at BiomarkIQ. It uses AI to estimate lung cancer risk based on lifestyle factors such as age, smoking history, and alcohol consumption. The full project integrates advanced biomarker data for highly precise health predictions.

Brain Network Analysis Comparison between Schizophrenia Patients and Healthy Control

Studied brain connectivity in schizophrenia patients using fMRI data and compared it with healthy individuals to understand differences in brain communication. Built brain networks and analyzed how different regions interact, finding weaker connections and lower efficiency in patients. These differences may help explain cognitive challenges associated with schizophrenia.

Phased Mythalation and Biomarkers Analysis

Small project to do statistical analysis of phased methylation patterns (PMPs) to assess their ability to differentiate tissue types.
